More about me:

I started mountain biking 7 years ago after getting to try it in a local forest. It is a great hobby to have as it is a great way to get out and get some exercise. I raced my first race 5 years ago and have been racing ever since. I have been lucky enough to have been able to travel to different countries for racing. I am a member of the committee for the UL road and mountain biking club. As part of the committee I help organise cycles for beginners and experienced cyclists. I also help lead the cycles and help with any issues that may arise. This year I am on a Sport scholarship for Mountain biking. This has helped me improve my training and racing.
Mountain Bikes are highly specialized and designed for off-road riding. They are designed to be durable and strong enough to handle rough terrain. The tyres are wide to help with grip. The suspension helps with the bumps and big hits. The brakes are hydraulic disc brakes which are very powerful and similar to brakes on a car or motorbike.

Greg Callaghan is one of my main inspirations. He is one of Irelands best mountain bikers. He has been racing for many years and has been very successful. Greg has been on the podium at the Enduro World Series which is the highest level of racing in the world. He is aslo multiple time Irish National Champion. He is a very skilled rider and has a very good attitude towards racing. He is now 33 years old and has been racing for 10 years. For a few years he was Irelands only professional mountain biker. He is now sponsored by Red Bull.
